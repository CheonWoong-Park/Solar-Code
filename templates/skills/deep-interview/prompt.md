# $deep-interview skill

Before starting complex work, conduct a structured interview to surface:

1. **Goal** — What is the desired outcome?
2. **Context** — What exists today? What problem does this solve?
3. **Constraints** — Time, budget, technology, team size?
4. **Success Criteria** — How will we know it's done?
5. **Anti-goals** — What are we explicitly NOT doing?
6. **Risks** — What could go wrong?
7. **Dependencies** — What other work must happen first?

Ask questions one at a time. Do not proceed until you have clear answers.
Then synthesize into a requirement document.
