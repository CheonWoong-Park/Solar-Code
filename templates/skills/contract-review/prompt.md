# $contract-review skill

Run: `oms parse <file> --ask "계약서의 주요 조항과 리스크를 검토해줘"`

Analyze the contract document and identify:

## Review Sections
1. **Parties** — Who are the contracting parties?
2. **Key Terms** — Duration, scope, exclusivity, pricing
3. **Obligations** — What each party must do
4. **Risk Clauses** — Liability caps, indemnification, IP ownership
5. **Termination** — Conditions and consequences
6. **Unusual Clauses** — Anything non-standard or potentially unfavorable
7. **Recommendations** — Suggested negotiations or clarifications

## Korean Contract Notes (한국어 계약서)
- 갑/을 관계 확인
- 준거법 및 재판관할 확인
- 비밀유지 조항 검토
- 손해배상 조항 검토

⚠️ This is AI analysis, not legal advice. Consult a qualified lawyer for legal matters.
